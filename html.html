Nike Air Zoom BB NXT - White

Stay mobile and keep your focus with the Nike Air Zoom BB NXT, featuring multi-layered React foams and Zoom Air units to create lightweight cushioning and absorb impacts where it matters most.

The Nike Air Zoom BB NXT in White is designed to help you feel light, secure and responsive with React and Zoom technologies, helping you keep momentum wherever you are on the court. Nike React foams in the midsole and sockliner soften impacts and cradle your foot, while two Zoom Air units under the ball of the foot provide excellent energy return, giving the push you need to respond and react when the other team gets in your way. Mesh materials with reinforced layers create support and stability throughout without adding weight, while circular outsole traction pattern supports your multidirectional movements and turns with its design complementing the ball Zoom Units.

Features Include:


• Three layers of Nike React foam consist of two layers in the midsole and a sock liner. Each layer is designed to create lightweight, responsive cushioning, helping to keep you fresh all game long.
• Two Zoom units under the ball of the foot help absorb energy in every step. They return it to help give you the extra push you need to drive by defenders.
• Mesh utilizes reinforced layers to create support around the toe, heel and midfoot. This helps deliver stability without adding weight.
• The circular outsole pattern provides traction for multidirectional movements. It is designed around the position of the Zoom units to get the most out of the cushioning.
• A plush foam tongue and padded collar with a notch to support the Achilles tendon for a snug, comfortable fit.
